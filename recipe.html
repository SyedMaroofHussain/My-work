Chicken Cheese Sandwich

Ingridients

  • 250 Gram Shredded Chicken
  • 1 tbsp Cheese Spread
  • 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Cream Cheese
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
  • Salt To Taste
  • 4 Slices Bread
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder (optional)

Method

  1. Take a bowl, add cooked and shredded chicken along with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, mayonnaise and some oregano, mix everything well.
  2. Once done, add cheese spread or cream cheese.
  3. Now take two slices of bread (any bread according to your preference), fill it with the mixture and add some veggies (optional), grill the sandwich until brown and crispy from both sides.
  4. Serve with your favourite dip and enjoy!
